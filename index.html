Part IV Reading Comprehension 阅读理解

In this research project, scholars studied the economic changes in a city after a new policy was introduced. Their approach was based on the analysis of data from different sources. They first identified the specific area where changes occurred and then estimated the percent of individuals who received a clear benefit. The researchers did not assume the result before completing the assessment. Instead, they looked for evidence. Several factors were involved, including the financial sector, the labour market, legal rules, and new legislation. The study also required a clear definition of each variable. The results indicated that the policy had a significant effect, but the interpretation was not simple. Some areas showed similar results, while others were different because they were derived from local conditions. In response, the local authority decided to create a clearer procedure for future policy decisions.
